Illustrated Sushi Alphabet Poster

I designed a tabloid-sized poster which showed off a type of sushi for each letter of the alphabet. The drawing for each other was done digitally in Photoshop and meant to evoke the aesthetic style of an Japanese ink painting.

Pineapple Typographic Poster

I designed a 11"x17" poster combining a botantical illustration of a pineapple with typography. The poster's copy "Travelers Welcomed" was based on the symbolic meaning of the pinepple (welcome or hospitality) along with an low-saturation color palette combined with the clear contrasting color for the typography.
