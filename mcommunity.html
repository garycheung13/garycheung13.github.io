Project Overview

Mcommunity

UX Research

This is an ongoing project projected to last about four months. I am working on a team with 4 other graduate students to conduct an in-depth UX evaluation of Mcommunity, an university wide directory of persons affilated with the school.

Project Summary

Client: Mcommunity, University of Michigan.
Duration: 4 months, projected.
Workform: Group of 5 graduate students.
Goal: Look for insights to identify current issues with the directory, and look for evidence to support future development.
Planned Work
  • Interaction Map — completed
  • Comparative Analysis
  • User Interviews – in-progress
  • Hueristic Evaluation
  • Personas
  • Usability Testing
