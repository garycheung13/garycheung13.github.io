Twitter Background Mockups

Graphic Design

I created four mockups for background images for The Arb, an botantical garden at the University of Michigan. Each one represents one of the four seasons with the message of "connecting with nature." Squrriels are a mainstay of campus life, and as such, I chose them to represent the connection which the manmade campus has with the natural world.
