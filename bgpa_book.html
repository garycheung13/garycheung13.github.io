Project Summary

Finding the Right Girls: Building Girls Protective Assets is a toolkit for designing girl-centered programs published by the Population Council, which is part of a larger collection of resources for designing girl-centered programs in developing countries. I was responsible for designing the layout of the book and doing the majority of the typesetting.
